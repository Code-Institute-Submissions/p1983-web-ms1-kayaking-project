Kayaking in Slovenia in the alpine Soča River Valley

Every year we run week-long kayaking trips to the beautiful Soča River valley in Bovec, Slovenia. This unspoiled turquoise river provides a perfect playground that caters for all grades of kayaking ability.

There are crystal clear pools and scenic stretches for absolute beginners leading right up to challenging grade IV rapids for more advanced paddlers.

The trips are split by ability; intermediate and beginner and both courses run during the same week of 24th May till 31st May 2020. So you can even come with a friend/family member of a different ability level. Explore the options below to find the trip that suits you best.

Kayaking Trip to Stunning Slovenia

A Holiday with a Difference. Date: 24th May 2020 – 31st May 2020. Soča River Valley Slovenia

How Much and What?

€1350 including all of the following:

  • 3 Excellent Meals a Day
  • Luxury Accommodation in Beautiful Bovec Surrounded by Mountain Views and within walking distance of local bars.
    (you can see accommodation here but please note we may change to similar accommodation depending on booking numbers. This is the minimum standard you can expect)
  • All Transport while there
  • All of the Gear and Equipment you require
  • 6 Days Fully Instructed Kayaking Classes on the Soča river. We have breakfast at 8.30am and leave for the river at 9.15am. You can expect to be back at your accommodation between 3.30pm and 5pm. You can choose to do shorter days or take days off as you wish.
  • River Passes
  • A Fun and Friendly Atmosphere.

For Whom?

The trip is suitable for complete beginners or those with little kayaking experience. Come on your own or in a group. We are very friendly and welcoming.

It’s like one of those all-inclusive ski trip thingies except that we include all the instruction and gear and equipment as well. Slovenia is one of the most beautiful places in the world to go kayaking.

This is the perfect course for those of you looking to go on a holiday with a difference. We will look after everything for you. Slovenia is an ideal place for beginners to kayak. Most of the day will be spent kayaking in the middle of the mountains with late afternoons spent relaxing in luxury surroundings, hiking the pristine valleys or engaging in any number of other activities available in Bovec.
