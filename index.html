What we do

Adventure Kayaking

Whether you want to go out for a leisurely paddle or run extreme rivers the Adventure Kayaking section has it all. This is where the true adventurers from the club come from. Every second weekend the river runners adventure away to parts far and wide to run rivers of all grades.

Open Canoeing

Open Canoeing (commonly know as Canadian Canoeing) at Rockhoppers has been going strong for years. The clubs’ focus has been to encourage people from all walks of life to get out on the water and enjoy the experience. We also run ‘Wild Camping’ canoe trips during the year

Freestyle Kayaking

Playboating is a discipline of whitewater kayaking canoeing where the paddler performs various technical moves in one place (a playspot), as opposed to downriver whitewater kayaking where the objective is running the river. Meet us at the Sluice, Dublin most Sundays.

RIVER TRIPS & EVENTS

River Trips

The club usually holds regular weekly river trips on a sunday. The Boyne and Liffey are main stays, the Annamoe, when it’s been raining also the Dargle, Analecky Brooke and Upper Liffey for the thrill seekers.

Events

Our next event will be posted soon. A list of all our weekly events can be found on the Events section of our Trips page and posted on our Facebook page.

Kayaking Trips Abroad

Each year, the club organises trips slighty further afield. So far the club has visited Uganda, Scotland, Wales and more recently the Grand Canyon. You can find accounts of these trips in our Trips section.
